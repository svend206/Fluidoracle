How it works

A consultation is a conversation, not a search. The platform gathers context, reasons from engineering literature, and tracks what happens after installation.

1

Describe your application

Operating conditions, system type, problem you're trying to solve. No account required to start.

2

Diagnostic questions

The platform asks targeted questions to understand constraints, failure history, and requirements.

3

Grounded recommendation

A specific recommendation with cited sources ‚Äî manufacturer catalogs and engineering standards, not general advice.

4

Outcome tracking

After installation, the platform follows up. Real-world performance data feeds back into future recommendations.

Component Performance Verification Protocol (CPVP)

The structured follow-up system that closes the loop between recommendations and real-world outcomes. Published as an open standard ‚Äî any platform or organization can implement it.

Design principles

Vendor-neutral

No manufacturer relationships influence recommendations. The knowledge base covers the full market.

Grounded in sources

Every recommendation cites the manufacturer catalog, standard, or technical literature it came from.

Outcome-driven

The platform tracks what happens after installation. Recommendations improve as real-world data accumulates.

Engineer-first

Free for engineers. No account required to get a recommendation. The platform earns trust through quality, not friction.
